# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Pizza lovers, foodies, friends, and families in Ahmedabad (Mani Nagar, Gota, Jagatpur, Bapunagar) looking for 100% pure vegetarian pizza, online ordering via Zomato/Swiggy, or dining reservations.

## Product Purpose

Establish Bro'etza as the premier 100% Veg pizza brand of Ahmedabad through a high-energy, immersive, interactive digital experience that highlights fresh daily dough, fire-baked crust, and bold local flavor.

## Positioning

"Pizza with 'Aaha!" — Ahmedabad's boldest 100% vegetarian pizza, baked over fire and delivered fresh.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: Order on Zomato / Order on Swiggy
- Secondary CTA: Explore Our Story / Order Online Modal
- Line remembered after 10s: The best 100% Veg pizza in Ahmedabad with that 'Aaha!' bite feeling.
- Belief ladder:
  1. Fresh dough prepared daily with pure vegetarian ingredients.
  2. Baked hot with real fire for unmatched crust and smoky depth.
  3. Loved locally across Mani Nagar, Gota, Jagatpur, and Bapunagar.
  4. Quick, hot delivery right to your doorstep.
- Proof on hand: Serving multiple active locations across Ahmedabad with upcoming expansion to Bapunagar.

## Brand Personality

Energetic, Bold, Flavorful. High-contrast hearth aesthetic with active typography and smooth motion.

## Anti-references

Corporate pizza chain templates, sterile white SaaS layouts, generic clip-art icons, and static stock photo cards.

## Design Principles

1. **Motion as Atmosphere**: Scroll-driven video scrub and kinetic typography build real hearth-side energy.
2. **High-Contrast Flavor**: Deep background tones paired with signature Bro'etza Red (`#BC3330`) and crisp display headers.
3. **Instant Action**: Direct access to delivery apps and reservation modals at every major scroll milestone.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast ratios, full keyboard modal navigation, and non-blocking `prefers-reduced-motion` fallbacks across all GSAP scroll timelines.
